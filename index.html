Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an app to use this?
No. Your digital card works instantly through a browser — no app required.
Does this work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. It works on both iOS and Android devices using NFC or link sharing.
Can I update my information later?
Yes. You can update your details anytime without changing your card.
Is this a one-time payment?
Yes. All plans are one-time payments — no monthly fees.
How long does it take to receive my card?
Typically 3–7 days depending on your selected card type and customization.
What makes Pro and Elite different?
Pro includes portfolio features and sample works. Elite is fully customized and limited per month.
What happens if someone doesn’t have NFC?
No problem. You can still share your card using a direct link.
